Terms and Conditions

We want to ensure transparency and fairness on our platform. By using GetYourTutor, you agree to the following terms:

  • Non-Refundable Payments: All payments made to GetYourTutor, including the Rs. 2500 connection fee for students and the Rs. 1000 registration fee for tutors, are non-refundable.
  • Confirmation of Connection: The tutor-student connection is confirmed only after admin approval and the payment of the Rs. 2500 connection fee by the student.
  • First Class Free Policy: Tutors are required to conduct the first class for free. Failure to provide this initial class without charge will result in an account ban. Future payment arrangements must be made directly between the student and the tutor.
  • Class Cancellations and Rescheduling: Tutors and students are responsible for coordinating and agreeing on their class schedules. Any cancellations or rescheduling must be mutually agreed upon.
  • Forum Conduct: All users must maintain respectful and appropriate communication in the forums. Inappropriate behavior, offensive language, or violations of forum rules will result in suspension or permanent removal from the platform.
  • Refunds: No refunds will be issued for any fees or payments once transactions are completed.
  • Platform Commission: GetYourTutor reserves the right to adjust platform fees or commissions at its discretion. Any changes will be communicated to users in advance.
  • Data Privacy: All user data is protected and will not be shared with third parties without explicit consent.
  • Tutor Responsibility and Profile Accuracy: Tutors must provide accurate and truthful information during registration. Providing misleading or false information will result in an account ban. Tutors are also responsible for ensuring their description aligns with their qualifications and abilities. GetYourTutor reserves the right to edit or remove any part of the tutor description without prior notice if the tutor cannot provide supporting evidence for their stated qualifications. Additionally, any phone numbers or contact information included in tutor descriptions will be removed.
  • Display of Tutor Information: By joining the platform, tutors agree to the public display of their name, subject, medium, town, description, and rating on their profile. Private information such as the A-level result sheet or NIC/ID photo will remain confidential and not be shown on the public profile.
  • Platform Liability: Once a tutor-student connection is established, GetYourTutor is not responsible for whether classes take place. Both parties are responsible for coordinating schedules, class plans, and payment details.
  • Admin Communication: The admin team will contact both the tutor and the student regarding payment details to ensure transparency and compliance with platform policies.
