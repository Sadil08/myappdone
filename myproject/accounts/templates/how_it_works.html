How It Works

Our simple process ensures that you connect with the best tutors in no time!

1. Search for Tutors

After registering as a student, navigate to your personalized dashboard and click on the "Search Teachers" link. You'll be able to search based on District, Teaching Medium (English, Sinhala, Tamil), and Subject.

Available teachers, along with their ratings, town, and other details, will be displayed for you to choose from.

2. Request and Connect

Click "Request a Teacher" once you've found the perfect tutor. Our admin team will contact both the student and teacher to confirm the first class, which is free of charge.

Once confirmed, the teacher will appear in your dashboard, and you will need to pay a one-time connection fee of Rs. 2500. This allows you to start learning with your selected tutor.

3. Enjoy Learning!

The first class is free, and you can decide with your tutor whether future classes will be held online or in person, based on your convenience.

Our tutors are high achievers, many of whom have excelled in their GCE A-Level exams, ensuring you're in great hands.

4. Get Extra Help with Forums

Need help outside of class? Head to our Forum where you can ask questions, share resources, and get answers from both teachers and fellow students.

Browse by subject to find the help you need anytime.
