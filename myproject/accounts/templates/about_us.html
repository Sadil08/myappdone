About Us!

Welcome to GetYourTutor, the platform designed to connect young tutors with students in a seamless, convenient way. Our goal is to make quality learning accessible to all while providing an opportunity for talented individuals to share their knowledge.

For Students

At GetYourTutor, we believe that education should be both personal and flexible. Here’s how it works for students:

  • Free Registration: Quick and easy!
  • Find Your Ideal Tutor: Search based on language, subject, and location.
  • Request a Tutor: Review profiles and send requests.
  • Admin Confirmation: Our team will contact you to confirm the tutor.
  • Flexible Class Options: Attend online or in person—your choice!
  • First Class Free: The first class is free for every new tutor.
  • Connection Fee: A one-time fee of Rs. 2500 after confirming the tutor.
  • Subject Forums: Ask questions, get answers from tutors and peers!

For Tutors

Are you an academic high-achiever looking to share your knowledge and earn money by teaching? Here’s how you can get started:

  • Register as a Tutor: Apply to be a tutor, reviewed by our admin team.
  • First Class Free: Show your skills to students—your first class is free.
  • Tutor Fee: Pay Rs. 1000 per student once connected.
  • Conduct Classes: Teach online or in person—it's your call.
  • Boost Your Rating: High ratings will help you attract more students.
  • Forum Participation: Answering questions in forums boosts visibility.
